    About Us

    TechJuice LLC came into existence with one core aim: To catapult Ghana and other African neighbors to the heights reached by Western and European countries in this forth Industrial technological disrupt. Various schemes have been developed to be the medium of this transition:

    TechJuice Academy

    TechJuice Academy is one of the most ideal environment that provides a great platform to learn and fully experience new technological development paradigms and become a person with relevant skills ready for the job market. We offer training for all levels of people ranging from Junior and Senior High school levers, passionate people with the desire to learn, Professionals who want to upgrade their skills or learn new technologies. Various modules have been designed to welcome people from various entry points of experience from amateur to professionals. Follow the link to see our various modules.

    TechJuice Software Solutions

    We seek to provide various levels of partnership and agreements with business owners who want to deploy and leverage on software systems to improve their mode of business operation efficiently. We provide services to Fin-Techs, the health sector, the educational sector and many others. We also provide staff training for companies who want its employees to become conversant with a software system they are preparing to deploy.

    TechJuice AI

    With Artificial Intelligence as the trail blazer technology in the recent technological disrupt, many companies and individuals are making efforts to also benefit from the powerful solutions that that Artificial Intelligence is bringing to business. We come in and make the whole situation much more better and convenient. We offer various Artificial Intelligence, Data Science and Machine learning business tailored solutions.
